## §1 Why this group is absorbed by Terafab

The **consciousness group** is hexa-chip's experimental axis. Per
`terafab.md` §1, consciousness is "normally owned by xAI Grok / Tesla
Dojo" — Terafab claims **in-house** ownership, which is the lightest claim
in the entire meta-domain because xAI already owns Grok and Tesla already
owns Dojo. The Terafab announce simply re-roofs the existing xAI / Tesla
training substrate under one captive entity.

This group rides alongside accelerator at **T3 product** in `terafab.md`
§4 — both groups produce *outputs* of the fab rather than process steps
inside it. The consciousness group has the **lowest current 🛸** of any
group (🛸6 vs the others' 🛸7), reflecting that consciousness-substrate
verbs are deliberately experimental at hexa-chip v1.0.0 with no empirical
claim. The +2 gap to 🛸8 is the smallest absolute target of any group.
